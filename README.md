        <p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Crack the eggs into a bowl.
</p>
        <p><strong>Step 2:</strong>  Add the              butter and mix.
        </p>
        <p><strong>Step 3:</strong>  Slowly add in          the flour while mixing.
        </p>
        <p><strong>Step 4:</strong>  Add the              sugar.
        </p>
        <p><strong>Step 5:</strong>  Cut the              apples and put them in the bowl.
        </p>
        <p><strong>Step 6:</strong> Mix.                 
        </p>
        <p><strong>Step 7:</strong>  Put the              dough in a form.
        </p>
        <p><strong>Step 8:</strong>  Turn the oven on 350 degrees.
        </p>
        <p><strong>Step 9:</strong> Put the form into the oven for 15 minutes.
        </p>
        <p><strong>Step 10:</strong>  Enjoy!
